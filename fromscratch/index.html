From Scratch took place at Gale-Martin Fine Art in New York City on January 4th, 2003. It included installations and performances by Nautical Almanac, Diagram A, Iron Lap, Gavin R. Russom & Delia R. Gonzalez, Cory Arcangel, LoVid, and Douglas Irving Repetto.
